Academic Excellence

At Mayuge High School, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that follows the Uganda National Curriculum, providing students with a solid foundation for higher education and career success. Our academic programs are designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention and support for each student. Our experienced faculty members use innovative teaching methods and modern educational technology to create engaging and effective learning experiences.

Curriculum Structure

Ordinary Level (S1-S4)

Our O-Level program provides a comprehensive foundation in core subjects, preparing students for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations.

Core Subjects

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • History
  • Geography
  • Religious Education

Additional Subjects

  • Computer Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Art & Design
  • Music
  • Physical Education
  • Technical Drawing
  • Home Economics
  • Commerce

Advanced Level (S5-S6)

Our A-Level program offers specialized education tracks that prepare students for the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) and university admission.

Science Track

  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • General Paper

Arts Track

  • Literature in English
  • History
  • Geography
  • Economics
  • General Paper

Commerce Track

  • Mathematics
  • Economics
  • Accounting
  • Business Studies
  • General Paper

Academic Facilities

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories with modern equipment for practical learning.

Computer Lab

Modern computer laboratory with high-speed internet for digital literacy and programming courses.

Library

Comprehensive library with extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

Smart Classrooms

Interactive whiteboards and multimedia equipment in all classrooms for enhanced learning.

Agriculture Farm

Practical agriculture learning facility with crop cultivation and animal husbandry sections.

Art Studio

Creative space for art and design classes with professional drawing and painting materials.

Assessment & Grading

Continuous Assessment

Regular tests, assignments, and projects throughout the term to monitor student progress and provide timely feedback.

  • Weekly quizzes and tests
  • Monthly examinations
  • Project-based assessments
  • Practical evaluations

Grading System

We follow the Uganda national grading system to ensure standardization and transparency in student evaluation.

A 80-100% Excellent
B 70-79% Very Good
C 60-69% Good
D 50-59% Satisfactory

Academic Support Services

Tutoring Program

Additional support for students who need extra help in specific subjects through peer and teacher tutoring.

Study Groups

Organized study sessions and group discussions to enhance collaborative learning and understanding.

Progress Monitoring

Regular progress reports and parent-teacher conferences to track and discuss student development.

Career Guidance

Professional counseling and guidance to help students make informed decisions about their future careers.
