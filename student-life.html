Vibrant Campus Life

At Mayuge High School, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our vibrant student life program offers numerous opportunities for personal growth, leadership development, and social interaction.

Students can participate in various clubs, sports teams, cultural activities, and community service projects that help develop well-rounded individuals prepared for future success.

Sports & Athletics

Our comprehensive sports program promotes physical fitness, teamwork, and competitive spirit among students.

Football

Both boys and girls football teams compete in inter-school tournaments and regional championships.

Basketball

Modern basketball court with competitive teams for both male and female students.

Volleyball

Popular sport among students with regular practice sessions and competitive matches.

Athletics

Track and field events including running, jumping, and throwing competitions.

Table Tennis

Indoor sport that develops quick reflexes and strategic thinking skills.

Chess

Mental sport that enhances critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

Clubs & Societies

Various clubs and societies cater to different interests and talents, fostering creativity and leadership skills.

Drama Club

Students explore acting, script writing, and stage production through theatrical performances.

Music Society

Choir and instrumental groups perform at school events and community functions.

Science Club

Hands-on experiments and scientific investigations beyond regular curriculum.

Debate Society

Developing public speaking and critical thinking skills through competitive debates.

Journalism Club

Student newspaper and media production covering school events and issues.

Environmental Club

Environmental conservation projects and awareness campaigns within the community.

Computer Club

Programming, web design, and digital literacy activities for tech-enthusiastic students.

Community Service

Volunteer activities and community outreach programs to help local communities.

Student Leadership

Student Council

Our student council provides opportunities for students to develop leadership skills and represent their peers in school governance. The council organizes events, advocates for student interests, and bridges communication between students and administration.

Council Positions:

  • Student President
  • Vice President
  • Secretary General
  • Treasury
  • Class Representatives
  • Club Presidents

Student Facilities

Dormitories

Comfortable boarding facilities with separate accommodations for boys and girls, supervised by qualified matrons and patrons.

Dining Hall

Spacious dining facility serving nutritious meals prepared by experienced catering staff.

Recreation Center

Indoor games room with table tennis, chess, and other recreational activities for leisure time.

Internet Access

Wi-Fi connectivity in designated areas for educational research and communication.

School Shop

On-campus store providing school supplies, snacks, and essential items for students.

Health Center

On-site medical facility with qualified nurse for basic health care and emergency response.
