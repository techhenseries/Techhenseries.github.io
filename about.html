About Mayuge High School

Home > About

Our Mission

To provide quality secondary education that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and leadership skills, preparing students to become responsible global citizens and future leaders in their communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading secondary school in Mayuge District, recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and holistic student development that transforms lives and communities.

Our History

Mayuge High School was established with a vision to provide quality secondary education to the youth of Mayuge District and surrounding areas. Since our founding, we have remained committed to academic excellence and character development.

Over the years, our school has grown from a small institution to one of the most respected secondary schools in the region. We have consistently maintained high academic standards while adapting to modern educational needs and technological advancements.

Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields including medicine, engineering, law, education, business, and public service, making significant contributions to their communities and the nation as a whole.
