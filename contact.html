Directions

The school is located in Mayuge District, Eastern Region of Uganda. It is easily accessible by public transport from major towns in the region.

  • From Kampala: Take the Jinja-Iganga highway and follow signs to Mayuge District
  • From Jinja: Use the Jinja-Kamuli road and turn towards Mayuge
  • Public transport: Regular buses and taxis operate from major towns to Mayuge

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school visiting hours?

+

Visiting hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Please contact the office in advance to schedule a visit.

How can I schedule a school tour?

+

School tours can be arranged by calling our admissions office at +256-XXX-XXXXXX or sending an email to admissions@mayugehighschool.edu.ug. We recommend scheduling at least 48 hours in advance.

What documents do I need for admission inquiries?

+

For admission inquiries, please bring academic transcripts, birth certificate, and any relevant previous school records. Our admissions team will guide you through the complete requirements.

Is there parking available for visitors?

+

Yes, we have designated parking areas for visitors near the main administration building. Please check in with security upon arrival.
