Join Our Community

We welcome students who are committed to academic excellence and personal growth. Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our challenging and supportive academic environment.

Mayuge High School is committed to providing equal opportunities for all qualified students regardless of their background. We encourage applications from students who demonstrate academic potential, good character, and a desire to learn.

Application Process

1

Obtain Application Form

Get the application form from the school office or download from our website. Forms are available throughout the year.

2

Complete Application

Fill out all required sections completely and attach all necessary documents as specified in the requirements.

3

Submit Documents

Submit your completed application with all supporting documents to the admissions office before the deadline.

4

Interview & Assessment

Attend the scheduled interview and any required assessments. We'll notify you of the date and time.

5

Admission Decision

Receive notification of admission decision. Successful candidates will receive admission letters with further instructions.

Admission Requirements

Senior One (S1) Admission

Academic Requirements

  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) certificate
  • Minimum aggregate of 12 points
  • Must have passed English and Mathematics
  • Birth certificate or statutory declaration of age

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • PLE results slip (original and copy)
  • Birth certificate or baptism card
  • 2 passport-size photographs
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Medical report from certified doctor

Senior Five (S5) Admission

Academic Requirements

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE)
  • Minimum of 5 passes at credit level
  • Must include English and Mathematics
  • Relevant subjects for chosen A-level track

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • UCE certificate (original and copy)
  • UCE results slip
  • 2 passport-size photographs
  • Transfer letter from O-level school
  • Character reference letter

Transfer Students

General Requirements

  • Academic transcripts from previous school
  • Disciplinary clearance certificate
  • Reason for transfer letter
  • School fees clearance certificate

Additional Documents

  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Parent/guardian consent letter
  • Medical examination report
  • Character reference from previous school

Important Dates

Application Period

Applications are accepted throughout the year. However, for S1 admission, applications should be submitted by the end of February.

Interview Dates

Interviews are conducted monthly. Specific dates will be communicated to applicants after submission of complete applications.

Term Begins

First term begins in early February, second term in May, and third term in September each year.

Admission Results

Admission decisions are communicated within two weeks of the interview. Successful candidates receive admission letters with joining instructions.
